Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need technical knowledge to work with you?

Not at all. I handle all the technical complexity - you just need to know your business goals. I translate your needs into working AI solutions and guide you through every step.

How long does a typical project take?

Most initial AI systems are live within 2-6 weeks depending on scope and complexity. I'll give you a precise timeline after your strategy call - and I stick to it.

What kinds of businesses do you work with?

I work primarily with small and mid-sized businesses - construction, trades, manufacturing, real estate, and professional services. If your business has processes that eat up time or money, there's likely an AI solution that fits.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely. I build with security-first architecture and follow industry best practices for data protection. Your data is never shared, sold, or used to train public models.

What's the investment to get started?

Pricing depends on the scope and complexity of your project. Book a free strategy call and I'll give you transparent, honest pricing - no surprises, no hidden fees.

What is the AI Audit and what does it cost?

The AI Audit is a free 45-minute working session - not a slide deck, not a generic assessment. I dig into your specific software stack, where your business is losing time, and what's holding back your growth. From there, we figure out what to do first, what to do later, and what's actually worth the investment. No templates, no predetermined outcomes - just a real conversation built around your situation.

Do I need to be technical to benefit from the AI Audit?

Not at all. The AI Audit is designed for business owners and operators, not IT teams. You don't need to understand how AI works - you just need to know your business. I handle the technical side and translate everything into plain-English recommendations focused on outcomes and ROI.
